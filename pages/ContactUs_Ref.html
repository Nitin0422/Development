Inspiration for Contact Us Page

The design of contact us page of this website was inspired from the website Weifeild Group. The page has three major sources of contacting with the company i.e. through phone, physical address or email. This concept was implemented in this website of Literary Delights with little to no modifications.

The design was implemented in the website with only slight modification in the visuals. This was only done to match the overall aesthetics of the website.
